# Code of Conduct

We are committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive community.

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Assume good intent and seek to understand before responding.
- No harassment, trolling, or personal attacks.
- Use inclusive language and be mindful of differing backgrounds and experiences.
- Respect the time and effort of maintainers and contributors.

If you observe or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to support@spectrayan.com. We will review and take appropriate action.

This Code of Conduct is inspired by the Contributor Covenant (v2.1).
